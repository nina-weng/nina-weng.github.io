description: "<ul>
<li>This project targets at using advanced explainability methods like LIME to further understand how image augmentation could help the learning process and increase accuracy in machine learning algorithm</li>
    <li>Introduce a method using the overlapping area of explanations and estimated ground truth to quantitatively evaluate the explanation result</li>
    <li>Propose a framework for examining the results of both raw and augmented data, which includes the data augmentation process, model training, explainability analysis and the quantitative evaluation</li>
    <li>Two experiments about MNIST and Dogs v.s. Cats classification are carried out with the proposed framework. After that, a correlation between data augmentation and explainability scores is discovered</li>
</ul>"
