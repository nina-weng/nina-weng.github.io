<em>Individual semester project, Neuroengineering Lab, Supervised by Dr. Stanisa Raspopovic</em> <br/>
<em>Zürich, Sep 2021 – Now</em>
<ul class='outside'>
    <li>The aim of this project is to build a trustworthy machine learning model that help clinicians treat chronic pain patients with better insights by giving to what degree the pain is caused by subjective (mental) or objective (sensory) indicators, hence avoid the abuse of opioids.</li>
    <li>To achieve the goal, the meaningful biomarkers for chronic pain intensity detection will be extracted from the EEG signal, Skin conductance, QST and questionnaires related to mental status, and several machine learning model will be explored with the emphasis on their explainabilities</li>
</ul>
